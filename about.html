About RoadVue

RoadVue is your trusted co-pilot, ensuring every drive is smooth, safe, and stress-free. Our advanced lane detection technology is designed to bring road safety to vehicles without built-in systems.

Key Features:

  • Dynamic Lane Tracking: Real-time overlays to keep you aligned with road lanes.
  • Customizable Settings: Adjust calibration, sensitivity, and features to match your driving style.
  • Hardware Compatibility: Integrates seamlessly with dashboard displays, monitors, or mobile apps.
  • Future-Ready Design: Scalable for additional features like traffic sign recognition and pedestrian detection.
